Information we collect

Newsletter

Our newsletter is managed through Beehiiv. When you subscribe, your email address is stored with Beehiiv and subject to their privacy policy.

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share your data with third parties except:

Cookies

Our website may use minimal cookies for essential functionality (e.g. remembering your light/dark mode preference). We do not use tracking cookies or third-party advertising cookies.

Your rights
