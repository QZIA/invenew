ABOUT INVENEW

INVENEW means invent new.

A founder-led media and Labs company focused on AI-native infrastructure, AI-native operations, and practical technology execution.

THE FOUNDER
QZ

Qamar Zia

Founder, INVENEW

I've spent 25 years building and operating cloud infrastructure, DevOps systems, and SaaS platforms. I've seen what enterprise AI actually looks like when it's running in production — and it looks nothing like the conference decks.

I started INVENEW because I kept seeing the same gap: there's plenty of AI strategy content, and plenty of narrow technical tutorials, but almost nothing focused on the infrastructure and operations layer — the part that actually determines whether AI works at scale in the enterprise.

INVENEW is my attempt to fill that gap. Operator-credible analysis, no hype, and practical framing for the leaders making real decisions.
