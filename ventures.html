OPPORTUNITY CRITERIA

What makes a strong INVENEW venture.

Not every gap is a business. These are the filters applied before an opportunity moves from Labs hypothesis to active venture.

  • The problem is infrastructure or operations-layer, not application or UI-layer
  • Enterprise teams are already spending money to work around it today
  • The AI-native version is meaningfully better than what exists, not just an LLM wrapper
  • The founding team has lived the problem, not just read about it
  • The category has at least one clear buyer persona with budget authority
  • INVENEW's existing distribution provides a real go-to-market advantage
  • It can be validated in Labs before significant capital is committed
  • The business model is clear, not dependent on a future monetisation hypothesis
